A cozy, small town bar, full of life.

The Stardrop Saloon may be a small town eatery, but it's got that big city taste! After enjoying the beautiful sights of the valley or exploring the depths of the mines, come wind down at the saloon with a cold beer and hot food! Come meet the staff below!

Meet Gus!

Gus lives, owns and works in The Stardrop Saloon. Gus is an avid cook and all the food is made by him and are original recipes!

Meet Emily!

Emily helps Gus out at the bar. Her friendly face is always accompanied by her great service!
